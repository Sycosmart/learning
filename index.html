01 · How I teach

We work on your real tasks, not a slideshow.

Your work, not a tutorial

No generic exercises. We open your actual inbox, your actual spreadsheet, your actual client intake process. Whatever is slow or repetitive in your week is the material we use.

Small sessions, one destination

Each program is a short series of focused sessions, not a semester. Every session builds on the last, and by the end you have one thing that actually works, not a folder of notes you'll forget by Friday.

You drive, I explain

You stay at the keyboard. I explain what's happening and why, in plain language, so you understand it well enough to change it later without me.

Where we work

Sessions run online, over video call with screen share. We work directly on your real tools, so nothing about the format gets in the way, and it keeps client data where it belongs, on your own machine. If you're based in Santa Cruz and would rather meet in person, that can be arranged at your office for the first session.
